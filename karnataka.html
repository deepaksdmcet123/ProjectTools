Welcome to Karnataka!

Karnataka, located in the southwestern region of India, is renowned for its rich cultural heritage, historical monuments, diverse landscapes, and the bustling city of Bangalore. Whether you're looking for ancient temples, beautiful waterfalls, or vibrant city life, Karnataka has something to offer everyone.

Top Tourist Places in Karnataka

  • Coorg

    Coorg, often referred to as the 'Scotland of India', is a picturesque hill station known for its coffee plantations, misty landscapes, and pleasant weather.

  • Hampi

    Hampi is a UNESCO World Heritage site, famous for its ancient temples, ruins, and the stunning landscape. It was once the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire.

  • Bandipur ational Park

    Located in the Western Ghats, Bandipur National Park is a renowned wildlife sanctuary that is home to tigers, elephants, and a variety of other species.

  • Mysore

    Mysore is famous for its royal heritage, grand palaces, and the spectacular Mysore Zoo. The Mysore Palace is a must-visit, especially during the Dussehra festival.
