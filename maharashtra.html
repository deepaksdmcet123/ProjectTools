Welcome to Maharashtra!

Maharashtra is known for its vibrant culture, historical landmarks, and beautiful landscapes. It's one of the most popular tourist destinations in India.

Top Tourist Places in Maharashtra

  • Gateway of India

    The Gateway of India is a historic monument built during the British Raj. It stands as a symbol of India's freedom struggle.

  • Ajanta and Ellora Caves

    The Ajanta and Ellora caves are rock-cut temples and monasteries that are UNESCO World Heritage Sites. They are famous for their exquisite sculptures and paintings.
